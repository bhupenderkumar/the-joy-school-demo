About The Joy School

The Joy School was founded in 1999 with a mission to create a supportive and inclusive community for students who require a more personalized approach to learning.

Our campus is designed to promote social, emotional, and academic growth, with state-of-the-art facilities and a range of extracurricular activities.

From our early childhood programs to our upper school, we foster a love of learning, creativity, and self-expression, helping our students become confident, capable, and compassionate individuals.
