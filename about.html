About The Joy School

The Joy School was founded in 1999 with a mission to create a supportive and inclusive community where students with learning differences can thrive.

Our campus is designed to foster a sense of belonging and curiosity, with state-of-the-art facilities and innovative learning spaces.

From our early childhood programs to our upper school curriculum, every aspect of The Joy School is tailored to meet the unique needs of our students, helping them build confidence, develop social skills, and achieve academic success.
