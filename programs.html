Our Programs

Lower School Program

Our lower school program is designed for students in grades K-5, focusing on foundational skills and social development.

Upper School Program

The upper school program serves students in grades 6-12, emphasizing academic rigor, independence, and preparation for post-secondary education.

Life Skills Program

This program focuses on practical skills, such as cooking, time management, and financial literacy, to promote independence and self-sufficiency.

Arts and Enrichment Program

We offer a range of elective courses and extracurricular activities, including music, art, drama, and sports, to nurture creativity and talent.

Summer Program

Our summer program provides a fun and engaging experience for students, with activities, field trips, and academic support to prevent summer learning loss.
