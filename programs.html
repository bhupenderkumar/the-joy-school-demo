Our Programs

Lower School Program

Our Lower School Program is designed for students in grades K-5, focusing on foundational skills in reading, writing, and mathematics, as well as social skills and emotional intelligence.

Upper School Program

The Upper School Program serves students in grades 6-12, offering a comprehensive curriculum that includes core subjects, electives, and extracurricular activities, all tailored to meet the individual needs of our students.

Summer Programs

The Joy School offers a range of summer programs and camps, providing opportunities for students to engage in fun and interactive learning experiences, from STEM activities to arts and crafts.

Professional Development

We are committed to ongoing professional development, ensuring our faculty and staff stay up-to-date with the latest research and best practices in supporting students with learning differences.

Parent Support

The Joy School is dedicated to supporting our parents and families, offering regular workshops, seminars, and one-on-one consultations to help them navigate their child's educational journey.
